# Roadmap

This roadmap defines the planned evolution of the Nawah Token ecosystem.
All roadmap items are driven by measurable outcomes and are directly linked to core product metrics.

Roadmap priorities are reviewed and adjusted based on data, not assumptions.

---

## Phase 1 – Foundation (Value Validation)

**Primary Metric:**  
Initiative Creation Rate

**Objective:**  
Validate that users can successfully create initiatives and derive real value from the platform.

### Focus Areas
- Reduce friction in initiative creation
- Improve clarity of purpose and flow
- Ensure basic Web3 readiness

### Planned Items
- Simplified initiative creation flow
- Clear initiative purpose and guidance
- Improved onboarding copy and structure
- Basic wallet connection support

**Success Criteria**
- Sustained increase in Initiative Creation Rate
- Stable Flow Completion Rate

---

## Phase 2 – Engagement (Community Interaction)

**Primary Metric:**  
Initiative Engagement Rate

**Secondary Metrics:**  
Return User Rate

**Objective:**  
Increase meaningful interaction with initiatives and encourage continued participation.

### Focus Areas
- Initiative visibility
- Community interaction
- Trust and relevance

### Planned Items
- Initiative interaction features (support, follow, share)
- Highlight active or impactful initiatives
- Improved initiative browsing experience

**Success Criteria**
- Increase in Initiative Engagement Rate
- Growth in returning users

---

## Phase 3 – Expansion (Web3 & Ecosystem Growth)

**Primary Metric:**  
Wallet Connection Rate

**Secondary Metrics:**  
Return User Rate

**Objective:**  
Expand decentralized participation and strengthen long-term ecosystem value.

### Focus Areas
- Web3 usability
- Deeper token utility
- Ecosystem integrations

### Planned Items
- Improved wallet connection flow and explanations
- Token-based interactions (where appropriate)
- Optional NFT or on-chain representations for initiatives

**Success Criteria**
- Improved Wallet Connection Rate
- Sustained Return User Rate

---

## Principles
- Roadmap items are subject to change based on metrics and experiments.
- No feature is added without a clear metric-driven justification.
- Experiments may precede full implementation when uncertainty exists.
